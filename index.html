Chocolate Chip Cookies

Pinned over 3 million times, these super soft and chewy chocolate chip cookies are the most popular cookie recipe on my website. Melted butter, more brown sugar than white sugar, and an extra egg yolk guarantee the chewiest chocolate chip cookie texture. Big chocolate chunks promise chocolate in every single bite. The flavor and texture of these cookies is unparalleled by any other recipe. No mixer required!

  • Time: 12 min
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients
2 1/4 cup flour 2 eggs
1tsp salt 1 tsp vanilla extract
1tsp baking soda 1 cup chocolate chips
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup sugar

Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Bake 12 min or until golden brown.

Chocolate Banana Smoothie

Power charge your day with this Chocolate Banana Smoothie made with velvety soy milk blended with banana! For additional creaminess & fun, top with whipped cream and colorful sprinkles.

  • Time: 5 min
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients
2 bananas
2 choc scoops
2 cups milk
2 scoops PB
1 cup ice cubes

Put the bananas, protein chocolate powder, peanut butter, ice cubes and milk in an electric blender/mixer. Blend until all the ingredients are combined and smooth.
